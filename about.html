Our Vision

About the School

Imaam Shaafici International Academy is one of the leading educational institutions in Mogadishu, Somalia. It has over 39 years of experience in education and has built a strong reputation for excellence and discipline.

The school operates 5 branches across Mogadishu, making quality education accessible to many students.

Our goal is to provide quality education that prepares students for a successful future, both academically and morally.
